Personal Life

Born in California, September 14th 2000. Austin grew up in a medium sized town off of Los Angelas California. Austin has a passion for gaming, and was his passion ever since he was around the age of three. At that age, Austin had a severe ear infection that brought him to the hospital. The hospital had a gaming room for kids to play in. This was the first time that Austin was exsposed to video games. He was blown away at the whole experience. Around the Fifth Grade, Austin already knew what he wanted to do as a career. That career was to create video games, and be a part of a industry that has greatly impacted his life for good.

Professional Life

Currently, Austin is attending school at the Cleveland Institute of Art. Here, he pursues his dream to become a Game Designer. He posts his art here for people to see.
